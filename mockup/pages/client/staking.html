Stake Polygon

Delegated MATIC Staking: Delegate your MATIC tokens to validators on Polygon PoS network. Minimum 1 MATIC. Earn ~5-6% APY. Checkpoint-based rewards (every ~30 minutes). Unstaking has an 80-checkpoint unbonding period (~3-4 days).
‚ö†Ô∏è Important Unstaking Information
Each cryptocurrency has different unstaking requirements:
  • Ethereum (ETH): 1-5 days depending on validator exit queue. No rewards during unstaking.
  • Avalanche (AVAX): Immediate after lock period expires. Rewards stop at lock period end.
  • Polygon (MATIC): 80 checkpoints (~3-4 days) unbonding period. No rewards during unbonding.
  • Unstaking cannot be cancelled once initiated
